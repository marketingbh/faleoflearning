About The Fale of Learning

The Fale of Learning was developed in New Zealand by Educator Pele Tui (pictured). With many of our pre-school children in lock down for the first time, their teachers are their parents and wider family.

Together with Tama and Louisa, the three are sharing videos of life at home and some lessons that we can continue to share with our children. Follow the journey and get in touch.
